Ritz Cracker Chicken

Ingredients

For the Chicken

  • 4 chicken breasts
  • 1.5 sleeves of Ritz crackers
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 Tablespoons salted butter
  • 2 Tablespoons oil

For the Gravy

  • 1 cup of whole milk
  • 1/2 cup half and half
  • 2 Tablespoons salted butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • 2 Tablespoons cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 Tablespoons corn starch
  • 1.5 chicken bouillon cubes

Description

Serving Size: 4
Time: 25 min

Steps

For the Chicken

  1. Crush 1.5 sleeves of Ritz crackers into big crumbs (can use a food processor or by hand) and spread onto a large plate.
  2. Crack the 2 eggs into a bowl and whisk.
  3. Heat a large pan on the stove on medium heat and add 2 Tbsps of salted butter and 2 Tbsps of oil.
  4. Cut the 4 chicken breasts into a few pieces (about 3 per breast). You may want to flatten the thicker parts to make them more even.
  5. Dip the chicken pieces into the egg mixture, and then into the Ritz, pressing each side firmly into the crumbs. Set each piece aside until they are all ready.
  6. Make sure the oil and butter are well mixed in the pan and add the chicken. Make sure the pieces are not touching if possible. You may need to do 2 batches.
  7. Cook until golden, about 5-7 minutes per side.

For the Gravy

  1. Set a medium pan on medium-low heat and add 1/2 a cup half and half, 2 Tbsps butter, and salt and pepper to taste. Crumble in the 1.5 chicken bouillon cubes. Stir regularly.
  2. In a separate bowl, add 1 cup of milk, 2 Tbsps flour, and 2 Tbsps corn starch. Use a fork and mix it until no clumps remain.
  3. Slowly pour the mixture into the pan, stirring as you go.
  4. Leave on heat, stirring regularly, until the sauce begins to thicken. Remove from heat and cover until ready to eat.
